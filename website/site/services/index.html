Our Services

At Stabilizing Behaviors Consultations, we specialize in providing comprehensive behavioral health services tailored to the unique needs of each child. Our compassionate, experienced team is dedicated to helping children and their families build skills, develop positive behaviors, and navigate challenges in a supportive, collaborative environment. From behavior modification to life skills development, each service we offer is designed to promote meaningful progress and foster independence.

Explore our range of services below to learn more about how we can support your family on this journey.

Behavior Modification and Reduction

Behavior modification is a crucial component of our approach at Stabilizing Behaviors Consultation, focusing on changing challenging behaviors while promoting positive alternatives.

Community Events

Our community events are designed to enhance social skills and encourage peer interaction among children.

Discrete Trial Training (DTT)

Discrete Trial Training is a structured teaching method that breaks learning into small, manageable tasks.

Feeding Therapy Program

Our feeding therapy program is designed to introduce children to new foods and expand their dietary preferences, particularly focusing on fruits and vegetables that they may initially dislike.

Life Skills Development

Our life skills development program focuses on equipping children with essential skills that promote independence and self-sufficiency.

Natural Environment Teaching

Natural Environment Teaching (NET) focuses on teaching skills in real-world contexts, allowing children to learn through their daily activities and interactions.

Parent Teaching and Support

Our parent teaching and support program is designed to empower families with the knowledge and skills necessary to reinforce learning at home.

Social Skills Group

Our social skills groups are designed to provide a supportive and interactive environment where children can practice and enhance their social interactions.
